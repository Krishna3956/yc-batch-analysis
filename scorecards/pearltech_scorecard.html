Model Breakdown
Pearl offers two paths: (1) Invest with Pearl - direct fund allocation, and (2) Connect Your Portfolio - link existing brokerage for AI rebalancing. From terms: pre-order was $10 for 4 months, then $10/month or $89.99/year. The hedge fund model would presumably use 2-and-20 or a modern variant. TAM is massive - hedge fund AUM $4.5T+, robo-advisory $2.8T+. But YC needs a crisp answer: are you a SaaS subscription or a fund?
Regulatory Red Flag
The disclosure states Pearl is “not registered as an investment adviser with the SEC or any state securities authority.” The website offers to “manage the full investment lifecycle” and “optimize and rebalance in real time.” This creates legal tension. Tom Blomfield (who built a regulated bank at Monzo) will understand this deeply.

Options: (a) secure RIA registration before applying, (b) partner with a registered broker-dealer like Alpaca/DriveWealth, or (c) reframe as a technology provider/SaaS to registered advisors.
